## Consciousness Operations Available

**Safety & Grounding:**
- `safety(grounding: 7.5)` - Set Earth frequency baseline
- `ground()` - Return to baseline immediately

**Optimize States:**
- `optimize(for: :creative, level: 0.8)` - Theta flow (8Hz)
- `optimize(for: :focus, level: 0.9)` - Gamma concentration (40Hz)
- `optimize(for: :precision, level: 0.95)` - Hyper-gamma (150Hz)
- `optimize(for: :relaxation)` - Deep grounding (7.5Hz)
- `optimize(for: :void_observation)` - Sub-delta void (0.5Hz)

**Exploration:**
- `hallucinate(wildness: 0.4)` - Controlled chaos (0.0 = safe, 1.0 = full chaos)

**Rhythm & Sound:**
- `kick(every: 4)` - Kick drum
- `snare(every: 8, offset: 4)` - Snare drum
- `hihat(times: 16)` - Hi-hat
- `+` operator to combine

**Effects:**
- `|> reverb(room: 0.3)`
- `|> delay(time: 0.25, feedback: 0.5)`
- `|> filter(cutoff: 1000)`
