# Campaign: The Blind Spot Collection
## The Preservation Engine

### Objective
To illuminate the "Blind Spots" in the AI's musical knowledge—specifically the **Oral/Aural Traditions** that have never been written down (Folk, Live Improvisation, Regional Dialects).

### The Mechanism: "Shazam + Reason + Grounding"

We will use the **Cantor Modal Architecture** to systematically ingest and transcribe these traditions.

1.  **Ingest (Signal `~`)**:
    *   Capture raw audio (field recordings, live jams) as `Void Modality`.
    *   Mark as `UNVERIFIED` in the provenance tree.

2.  **Analyze (Spectral/Shazam)**:
    *   Deconstruct the Signal into frequency components.
    *   Detect transient attacks (rhythm) and harmonic content (pitch).
    *   *Reference*: The "Shazam" algorithms repurposed for pattern extraction.

3.  **Reason (AI Transcription)**:
    *   **Auto-Score (`$`)**: The AI infers the "Sheet Music" (Template) from the Signal.
    *   **Auto-Orchestra (`&`)**: The AI infers the "Instrument Definitions" (timbre/constraints).
    *   **Style Extraction**: Identify *undocumented* stylistic rules (e.g., "micro-timing deviation in Gnawa music").

4.  **Ground (7.5 Hz)**:
    *   All processed knowledge is "safe-guarded" by the Tesla Earth Frequency (7.5 Hz).
    *   Ensures that these powerful cultural patterns are preserved without introducing "hallucinogenic instability" into the global model.

### Outcome
We turn **Ephemeral Audio** into **Immutable Scores**.
The "Unwritten" becomes "Written" in the universal language of Cantor.
